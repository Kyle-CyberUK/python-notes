# Comparison Operators

## Overview
- Comparison operators are used to compare 2 values and receive a Boolean result (True or False).
- They are essential for decision making and validation.

## Key Concepts
- `==` - Equal to
- `!=` - Not equal to
- `<` - Less than
- `>` - Greater than
- `<=` - Less than or equal to
- `>=` - Greater than or equal to

## Example
```
age = 18
if age >= 18:
	print("You can vote")
else:
	print("You're too young to vote")
